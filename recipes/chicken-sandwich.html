Yummy Chicken Sandwich!

This simple yet tasty chicken sandwich is my go to snack/meal.

Ingridients

  • Two slices of Bread
  • Light Mayo
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Mixed Spices
  1. Toast the Two loafs of Bread
  2. Mix a bit of yogurt, ligh mayo and sriracha as the base sauce
  3. Grill the chicken with the mixed spices added
  4. Spread the sauce on the loafs and add the chicken in-between the loafs

Your sandwich is ready. Enjoy!
