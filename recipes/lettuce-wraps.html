Lettuce Wraps

Lettuce wraps are great, as they are fast to make and incredibly healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet MAGGI Your Meal Your Way Cottage Pie
  • 1 Tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 400 Grams Pork, Mince, Raw
  • 230 Grams Can Water Chestnutes, Chopped
  • 230 Grams Bamboo Shoots
  • 2 Tablespoon Sweet Chilli Sauce
  • 2 Unit Spring Onion, Sliced
  • 1 Unit Iceberg Lettuce, outer leaves cut into cups
  • Some Chopped Peanuts
  • Some Chopped Corriander Leaves

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat a large frying pan, add oil, and cook mince for 5 minutes or until browned.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add water chestnuts, bamboo shoots and MAGGI Your Meal Your Way Chow Mein, sweet chilli sauce and cook for 5 minutes or until heated through. Fold through spring onions.

  3. Step 3

    4. Spoon mince mixture into lettuce cups to serve. Top with peanuts and coriander if desired.

Enjoy the tasty and healthy wraps!!
